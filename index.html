Welcome

A Different Idea of Luxury

Kaveri Palaces was never built as a hotel brand — it grew, one restored palace at a time, out of a simple belief: that the grandest way to experience India is to actually live inside its history, not just look at it from a bus window.

Each of our thirty-one addresses was once a royal residence, a merchant's mansion or a coastal fort. We restore the stone, the frescoes and the courtyards exactly as they were, then quietly add the comforts a modern traveller expects — without ever letting the two feel at odds with each other.
